Asheville Homeschool Culture Club

A place for homeschoolers to learn more about the people and places of our world.

The Asheville Homeschool Culture Club is open to any family. Space is limited and enrollment in the club is contigent upon approval and payment of annual dues. If we reach capacity for our current facilities, we will gladly put you on a wait list.

The annual dues for AHCC are nominal and only cover costs of our facility rental and supplies. Dues are calculated once we have our enrollment numbers for the school year. In addition to dues, families will also have the cost of participating in the pot luck. Families usually only bring one dish. Please be sure to bring sufficient quantity of that dish so that everyone may get a helping of an 1/8 cup size portion.
