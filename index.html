Asheville Homeschool Culture Club

A place for homeschoolers to learn more about the people and places of our world.


Asheville Homeschool Culture Club (AHCC) is a group of Asheville area homeschoolers who gather monthly to share presentations about a topic country. At the meeting, the homeschool children present information to the group about a specific aspect of that month's country. After the presentations, we share in a pot luck with foods from the chosen country.
