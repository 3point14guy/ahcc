Asheville Homeschool Culture Club

A place for homeschoolers to learn more about the people and places of our world.





Asheville Homeschool Culture Club (AHCC) is a group of Asheville area homeschoolers who gather monthly to share presentations about a topic country. At the meeting, the homeschool children present information to the group about a specific aspect of that month's country. After the presentations, we share in a pot luck with foods from the topic country.













Presentations take many forms; from the typical speech style, to interactive games, to demos that include audience participation, to interviews conducted in person or via Skype.


Presentations are for home schoolers age 5 and above. Younger siblings are welcome to watch the presentatipons or participate in an older siblings presentation.









The Asheville Homeschool Culture Club is open to any family. Space is limited and enrollment in the club is contigent upon approval and payment of annual dues. If we reach capacity for our current facilities, we will gladly put you on a wait list.


The annual dues for AHCC are only $20 per child and cover costs of our facility rental and supplies. In addition to dues, guardians all chip in with set up, clean up, and hosting the pot luck. Families usually only bring one dish. Please be sure to bring sufficient quantity of that dish so that everyone may get a helping.
